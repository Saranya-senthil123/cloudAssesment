Admission Details

Welcome to the Government College admission portal for the academic year 2025-26. Admissions are open for undergraduate and postgraduate courses in arts, science, commerce, and more.

Application Deadline: August 31, 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed 12th grade or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Minimum aggregate score of 50% (general category).
  • Minimum aggregate score of 45% (reserved categories).
  • For postgraduate courses, a relevant bachelor's degree is required.
  • Applicant must be an Indian national.

Documents Required

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets and Certificates
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Proof of Residence (Aadhar card/Passport)
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized Photographs (4 copies)

Certificates

  • Proof that you have left your previous school/college
  • Required if you are moving from a different university or board
  • Aadhar Card, Passport, Voter ID, or any government-issued ID
  • Proof of address like utility bill, ration card, or Aadhar
  • Birth Certificate (sometimes required)
